Our Story

MEG@VPS SECURITY AGENCY, INC. Founded in 2009, was conceptualized by group of security experts and security practitioners to implement security processes and procedures that promote the safest possible environment, minimize disruption and reduce potential loss of life and assets MEG@VPS was designed in protecting its clients through management expertise and competency. Retired Colonel Franc Hermoso leads the management team of security professionals. Their exposure to highly skilled professional communities enable them to develop a comprehensive facility security design as well as hiring appropriate staff and implementing essential technologies to critically meet the challenges from acts of terrorism, theft, fire, fraud, vandalism and other risks that became a daily reality.

Our Mission

To lead the industry in the provision of quality services through sustainable training and development services and in the pursuit of modern outlook through research and technology development.

Our Vision

To be the leader in security services in the Philippines in terms of quality, quantity, cost effectiveness, innovation, clients and employees satisfaction in the light of changing economic and business condition.
