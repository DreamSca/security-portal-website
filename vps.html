Our Story

The VPS Security Agency Inc. stands for Veterans Philippine Scouts Security Agency, Incorporated. It is a group of professionals who combine their experience in the field of Security and customer service, to design a system of procedure and security process that will provide the safest environment, reduce disruption and prevent the potential danger to the life and asset of their clienteles. At the helm of its operation is Eddimar O. Arcena, a Management Engineer and graduate of Law, combined by his years of experience as government employee understands the need for competent and professional security personnel that will be a potent counter measure against acts of terrorism, theft, fire, fraud, vandalism and other risk that had become a day to day reality. This is what VPS has to offer.

Our Mission

To satisfy the needs of our clients make them feel the security and safety of their lives and property because of our presence. That we offer a different kind of service, because we do it with love, respect and understanding of their needs and with full consideration to their expectations.

Our Vision

To be at the lead in the industry and make our name synonymous to good, quality service, by providing our clients effective cost efficient innovations in manner of securing their life and property through the utilization of modern technology abreast with the present economic condition.
